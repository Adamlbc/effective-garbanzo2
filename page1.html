2. Nvidia GeForce RTX 5060 Ti

This graphics card offers a good generational upgrade from the 4060 Ti. This GPU has a higher VRAM and faster memory. Overall the RTX 5060 Ti is a good mid level GPU, although the performence doesn't match the RTX 5060 or 4070 at 4K, it still is a good choice for modern work or gaming.

3. AMD Radeon RX 9070 XT

The RX 9070 XT is a really good graphics card for the price. It runs games really well, especially at 1440p, and it’s also good for things like AI and ray tracing. Some people even say it can compete with Nvidia cards like the RTX 5070. It also stays pretty cool when you’re using it, which is nice. The only problem is that it might be hard to find in stock. Overall, it’s a great GPU

4. Nvidia GeForce RTX 5070 Ti

The Nvidia GeForce RTX 5070 Ti is regarded as a high-end GPU that offers a solid upgrade from the RTX 4070 Ti. It is praised for its performance at 1440p and 1080p, making it a good choice for extreme tasks. The card features 16GB of VRAM and is considered the "Goldilocks GPU" in Nvidia's lineup, striking a balance between performance and price.
